Current Research

Applications of Partial Differential Equations

I am currently working on research in partial differential equations under the mentorship of Harbir Antil.
My research involves numerics with a fractional order PDE, which has applications in quantum computing.

I recently gave a Minisymposium talk on my work at the SIAM Central States Section Meeting. You can find the slides here.

Scientific Software

I am also working on a library called ChebTools in collaboration with Ian Bell, who is affilated with NIST.

The goal of this library is to provide a C++/Python library for working with Chebyshev Polynomial approximations of functions. My work has specifically focused on creating the 2-D version of the library with specific focus on root finding.

You can find the master branch of the library here, and you can find my fork of the project here. I also gave a short talk on this research at the George Mason University Applied and Computational Math Seminar. The slides can be found here.
